Our Program

Community Dialogues

Every 3rd week of every month, we plan for community meetings where we bring the community together to speak on daily issues that affects them.

We engage on matters that are beneficial to the community.

We also invite relevant authorities who are concerned with the topic that is being discussed.

Psycho-Social Support

We currently have a counsellor at the center who offer counselling session to victims and also to our member who are in need.

The services are free of charge.

The sessions are usually conducted from Monday-Friday, as from 8am-3pm.

Legal Services

We offer free legal advice to the community.

We have a lawyer at the center provided by International Justice Mission.

The services and sessions are conducted on Wensday as from 10am-5pm.
